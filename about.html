About Sadiqa Akhter

A career built where learning, art, and technology meet.

Sadiqa's work has grown from visual storytelling into a founder-led education and creative-tech ecosystem: children's publishing, STEM programs, AI literacy, teacher training, and global creative direction.

Professional Journey

From illustrator to learning ecosystem builder.

This timeline gives visitors a simple way to understand the full arc: not separate careers, but one connected path toward creative education leadership.

01

Children's Book Illustrator

Built deep experience in character design, picture-book storytelling, formatting, and publishing-ready creative work.

02

Creative Director

Expanded from individual projects into art direction, visual systems, author support, and international creative delivery.

03

Founder of Source II Studio

04

05

STEM Educator

Developed workshops, mentorship experiences, coding inspiration, space science engagement, and classroom innovation programs.

06

AI Education Leader

Integrated AI literacy, responsible tools, Google educator practice, Gemini credentials, and future-skills learning into her education work.

07

Speaker & Workshop Presenter

Supports schools, conferences, teachers, learners, and organizations with practical sessions around creativity, STEM, AI, and innovation.

08

Future Vision

Building learning worlds where children, educators, authors, and organizations can use technology with imagination and purpose.
